The Shepherd

About Father Charles

Father Charles is a devoted Catholic priest and community servant, known for his compassionate heart and unwavering faith. For over two decades he has walked alongside his congregation through every season of life — joy, sorrow, breakthrough, and storm.

His ministry extends beyond the parish walls: championing education for underprivileged children, holding weekly prayer vigils, and bringing the sacraments to those who cannot come to him.

He believes that every prayer matters, every child deserves a future, and that no one should walk their road alone.

The greatest thing we can do for another person is to believe in them — in the grace that God has hidden inside them — before they believe it themselves.

— Father Charles
